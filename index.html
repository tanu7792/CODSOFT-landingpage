BURGER HAUEM

A burger is a popular sandwich consisting of a cooked patty—typically made from ground beef, chicken, or plant-based alternatives—placed inside a sliced bun. It is often topped with ingredients like lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, and condiments such as ketchup, mayonnaise, or mustard. Some variations include bacon, fried eggs, or specialty sauces for added flavor. Burgers can be grilled, pan-fried, or flame-broiled and are commonly served with fries, coleslaw, or a side of salad. They are a staple in fast food and casual dining, loved for their juicy, savory taste and endless customization options.
